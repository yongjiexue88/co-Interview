On paper.</li><li class="text-gray-300">Practice: Reverse List, Detect Cycle</li></ul><h4 class="text-lg font-bold mb-6 text-white">Stacks &amp; Queues</h4><ul class="mb-6 space-y-2 pl-6"><li class="text-gray-300">Why they matter: Control the order of execution. Don’t forget to visit the sets.</li><li class="text-gray-300">Practice: Number of Islands, Course Schedule</li></ul><h4 class="text-lg font-bold mb-6 text-white">Hash Tables</h4><ul class="mb-6 space-y-2 pl-6"><li class="text-gray-300">Why they matter: Fast lookup. Trade time for space.</li><li class="text-gray-300">Tips: Talk through collisions. Don’t treat it like a magic box.</li><li class="text-gray-300">Practice: Two Sum, Longest Palindrome</li></ul><h3 class="text-xl font-bold mb-6 text-white">Quick Complexity Math So You Don’t Ramble</h3><p class="mb-6 text-gray-300 leading-relaxed">You don’t need to be a mathematician.
